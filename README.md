# SK Barangay Bussot Website

Official website of the Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) of Barangay Bussot.

## About
The Sangguniang Kabataan of Barangay Bussot serves as the official youth governing body dedicated to developing empowered, disciplined, and socially responsible youth leaders through inclusive programs and community-driven initiatives.

## Vision
An empowered, united, and progressive youth sector of Barangay Bussot actively participating in nation-building and community development.

## Mission
To promote youth leadership, integrity, and volunteerism by implementing responsive programs in education, sports, health, culture, and environmental stewardship, fostering active participation and holistic growth of the youth.
